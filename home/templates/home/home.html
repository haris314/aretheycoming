aretheycoming

A platform to organize events

Especially designed for school/college students to manage bunks and overall lecture attendance

How to use this platform?

  1. Sign up.
  2. Search for groups you want to join.
  3. Send a join request to the group.
  4. Admins of the group will see it and they will decide whether you will become part of the group or not.
  5. Once you are accepted, you can vote in that group's events or create your own events in the group which will be visible to all the members of that group.
  6. An admin of the group is authorized to promote other members to admin or remove them from the gorup.
  7. Alternatively, you can also create your own groups.
